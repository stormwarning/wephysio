The faster we can get you back to what you do, the happier we both are

WePhysio has been providing Edmontonians experienced physical therapy services for over 26 years. We believe the ideal way to recover or perform is to feel encouraged to achieve your best. We want all of you to feel like you are free to realize your best health and best performance.

Wellness is finding and maintaining your optimal state of health in both body and mind. Whatever injury or long-term pain is preventing you from being whole is what we want to help you fix.

Together we remove the mystery surrounding your injury and run performance. Get the hard data to simplify your path to performance.
