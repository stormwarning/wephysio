Performance

At WePhysio we see performance as the journey towards higher athletic and sport-related goals. Wellness is the underpinning of performance. Once your health is optimal, we can help you achieve milestones in your athletic pursuits. Or help you start on the path towards an exercise or sport regime. We offer several very detailed performance assessments. These are designed to measure specific aspects of performance related to your sport.

Run Assessment

A detailed check of your bodyâ€™s structure, biomechanics, strength, and flexibility. Find out how your body is designed to move given its current status and then, depending on your goals, work on objectives to improve performance.

Cycling Assessment

An assessment of bike fit and power production as well as symmetry of power production are just the beginning. Couple this with a sub-maximal or maximal fitness test and you have all the test/retest data you need to maximize performance.

Roller Derby/Skate Assessment

A sub-maximal fitness test, a review of movement, stability, strength, and flexibility as well as edge control and stride. We put the glide in your stride.

3-D Gait

3D Gait Analysis Asessment

The most advanced gait analysis system on the planet. 3D Gait is an innovative health solution helping prevent and treat running and walking injuries.

This system is normally only available in larger research centres. WePhysio is one of less than a dozen clinics in the world to have a 3D Gait Analysis System available to clients. Full gait analysis includes three separate portions:

  1. A one-on-one assessment with Craig Brososky
  2. The 3D Gait data capture where your own walking and/or running stride is captured and examined in the state-of-the-art system
  3. A follow up visit to see how your findings have assisted your running development
